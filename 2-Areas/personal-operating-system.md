# Standard

The system should reduce cognitive load. Capture can be messy, but review should clarify ownership, next action, and where the information belongs.

# Practices

* Capture raw information in [Inbox](/0-Inbox/).
* Convert actionable outcomes into [Projects](/1-Projects/).
* Convert ongoing responsibilities into [Areas](/2-Areas/).
* Convert reusable material into [Resources](/3-Resources/).
* Move inactive material into [Archive](/4-Archive/).
