# Purpose

MindHive exists to make knowledge durable, navigable, and usable by both humans and agents.

# Principles

* The vault is a working system, not a dumping ground.
* PARA determines where information lives.
* MOCs determine how thinking is navigated.
* Links make relationships explicit.
* OKF keeps the bundle portable and machine-readable.
* Decisions and assumptions should be visible when they affect future work.

# Operating Contract

Humans should capture intent, review direction, and decide priorities.

Agents should preserve structure, expose assumptions, update links, and leave work resumable.
